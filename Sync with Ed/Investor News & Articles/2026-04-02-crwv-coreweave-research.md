## What Is CoreWeave?

CoreWeave is an AI cloud infrastructure company — think of them as the "landlord" of GPU computing power. They don't build AI models themselves. They build and operate massive data centers packed with NVIDIA GPUs, then rent that compute power to companies building AI.

**The elevator pitch:** If AI is the gold rush, CoreWeave is selling the shovels.

**Key facts:**

- Founded 2017 in Livingston, NJ — originally a crypto mining company, pivoted to AI cloud after the 2018 crypto crash
- 32 data centers, 250,000+ GPUs
- Built a $1.6B supercomputer for NVIDIA in Plano, TX — NVIDIA called it the fastest AI supercomputer in the world
- Powers OpenAI and Microsoft with large-scale GPU cloud
- Used Meta contracts as collateral for an $8.5B loan in Feb 2026
- Products: GPU Compute, CPU Compute, Storage, Networking, Managed Kubernetes, Bare Metal Servers
- Solutions: AI Model Training, AI Inference, VFX & Rendering

**Customers include:** OpenAI, Microsoft, Meta, NVIDIA, and dozens of AI labs and enterprises

## GEX Breakdown (Educational)

| GEX | What Dealers Do | Market Behavior |
|-----|----------------|-----------------|
| Positive (+) | Dealers are LONG gamma | They suppress moves. Buy dips, sell rips. Market mean-reverts. Volatility dampened. |
| Negative (-) | Dealers are SHORT gamma | They amplify moves. Sell into drops, buy into rips. Market trends harder. Volatility unleashed. |

**SPY GEX: -199.4M = dealers are short gamma = any move gets AMPLIFIED**

This is why Ed says "big moves could come either way." The shock absorbers are off. When the market picks a direction, it could run hard.
